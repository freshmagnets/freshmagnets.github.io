Photo Magnet Gift Ideas for Every Occasion

The best gifts are personal, useful and don't break the bank. A custom photo magnet is all three — and it lives where people actually see it every day.

Updated June 20265 min read

Photographs make great gifts, but framed prints are expensive and end up on a shelf in the back room. A magnet does the same emotional job for a fraction of the cost and ends up somewhere everyone passes a dozen times a day: the fridge. Below are ideas for the occasions people gift them most.

🎂 Birthdays

Print a favorite memory with the birthday person — a trip, a party, an inside-joke photo — and you've turned a card-and-cash gift into something they'll keep. A small set works as a stocking-style add-on, or hand them out as party favors so every guest leaves with a snapshot. Browse birthday photo magnets.

🍼 Baby showers & new parents

New parents take a thousand photos and print almost none of them. A pack of magnets is a gentle nudge to get those first smiles onto the fridge. Sonogram announcements and "meet the baby" photos both work beautifully. See baby shower photo magnets.

🎓 Graduations

Cap-and-gown portraits, the whole-family shot at the ceremony, or a four-years-of-friends collage all make a graduation gift that means something. They're also an easy keepsake to mail to relatives who couldn't attend. Shop graduation photo magnets.

🐶 Pet lovers

For a lot of people, the pet is the family. A magnet of a dog or cat is a guaranteed smile — and the tight round crop is made for a good pet portrait. They're a thoughtful "just because" gift and a comforting keepsake. See pet photo magnets.

💍 Weddings & engagements

Beyond favors, a single beautifully printed magnet of the couple makes a sweet engagement or anniversary gift. Pair it with the full wedding photo magnets range if you're helping plan favors too — our wedding guide has the details.

🎄 Holidays

Family holiday photos are practically made for magnets. Gift a set to grandparents, or send your annual photo as a magnet instead of a card that gets tossed in January. Browse Christmas photo magnets.

🕊️ Remembrance

A small magnet of someone you've lost — a parent, a grandparent, a beloved pet — keeps them close in the most ordinary, comforting way: on the kitchen fridge, part of daily life. See memorial photo magnets.

Which product should you choose?

For most gifts, start with the versatile square custom photo magnets. Choose the rectangular fridge photo magnets for upright portraits, and the round personalized photo magnets when you want something playful for faces and pets. Our size guide breaks down the differences.

Gifting for a crowd? Packs come in 6, 9, 12 and 15 — combine them or go bulk for events.
